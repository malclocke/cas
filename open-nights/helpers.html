The table below lists the open nights for 2014 and the names of the society members who have volunteered to assist with each night.

The society desperately needs more members to volunteer to help with open nights. If you'd like to help, please contact the open night organisers at bookings@cas.org.nz and let them know which night or nights you are available. Helpers must be society members, but all experience levels are welcome. Open nights are a great time to get training and experience using the society's telescopes as there are always experienced members on hand to help.
