StarDate SI

Stardate SI is an annual star party held at a dark sky site at Staveley in inland Canterbury between Methven and Geraldine. In 2015 Stardate SI takes place between Fri Feb 20th and Mon Feb 23rd.

Stardate SI is held at a hostel and campsite. It has the following facilities:

  • Full toilet & showers
  • Bunkrooms
  • An auditorium Kitchens with shared fridges and freezers
  • A large cafeteria
  • Plenty of space for tents and caravans

The viewing area has excellent horizons in all directions, and space for many telescopes.

The surrounding countryside is beautiful, with fine walks through beech forests.

For more details and registration, please visit the camp website
